# How Parents Pay School Fees Online

---

## You need

- Invoice number **OR** payment link from school (WhatsApp/SMS)
- Student **admission number**
- Mobile Money phone or bank account (if paying instantly)

---

## Method 1 — Payment link (easiest)

1. Tap link from school: `https://e-digitex.com/pay/...`
2. See student name and **balance due**
3. Choose:
   - **Pay instantly** — follow phone prompt or checkout page
   - **Upload proof** — if you already paid elsewhere

---

## Method 2 — Find invoice

1. Go to **https://e-digitex.com/pay**
2. Enter invoice number + admission number
3. Continue to payment page

---

## Pay instantly

1. Enter your name and phone (243...)
2. Enter amount (full or partial if allowed)
3. Select Orange / Airtel / M-Pesa
4. Confirm on your phone or complete checkout page
5. Success message + reference number

---

## Upload proof

1. After paying at agent, open same pay link
2. Tab **Upload proof**
3. Enter date, time, transaction ID from SMS
4. Attach photo of receipt
5. Wait for school confirmation (usually 1–2 business days)
