# Manual Payment Proof — Parent Guide

Use this when you **already paid** at a Mobile Money agent, bank, or school office and need the school to **confirm** your payment.

---

## Steps

1. Open the payment link from the school (or go to `/pay` and find your invoice).
2. Click tab **Upload proof**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Your name** and **phone number**
   - **Amount paid** (must not exceed balance due)
   - **Payment method** (Orange Money, bank transfer, etc.)
   - **Date & time paid** — exact time on receipt
   - **Transaction reference** — ID from SMS or receipt (required)
   - **Receipt photo** — screenshot or PDF (recommended)
4. Click **Submit for verification**.

---

## What happens next?

- Status: **Pending review**
- School accountant checks receipt against Mobile Money statement
- If approved → invoice shows **Paid**
- If rejected → contact school with correct details

---

## Tips for fast approval

- Use clear photo of full SMS or bank slip
- Transaction ID must match exactly
- Amount must match what you paid
- Include student name in notes if paying for multiple children
